Course 1

Course information

In the intricate dance of pixels and code, web design and development intertwine to craft digital masterpieces that transcend mere functionality, becoming portals to boundless creativity and innovation. Like architects of the virtual realm, designers sculpt user experiences with precision and flair, while developers breathe life into these visions, weaving intricate algorithms and technologies into seamless symphonies of interactivity. Together, they harmonize aesthetics with functionality, transforming blank screens into vibrant canvases that engage, inspire, and connect users across the vast expanse of the internet. In this dynamic realm where art meets technology, every line of code and stroke of design is a brushstroke in the ever-evolving tapestry of the digital age.

Course Code: WEB-101

Prerequiste: CP-101, Net 101
